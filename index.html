About Disneyland

Disneyland is a world-renowned theme park located in Anaheim, California. It is known for its magical atmosphere, thrilling rides, and beloved Disney characters.

Opened in 1955, Disneyland is divided into different themed areas, including Adventureland, Fantasyland, Tomorrowland, and more. It attracts millions of visitors each year from all over the world.
